About Us

The Mary Emmanson Foundation is a non-profit organisation registered to work in partnership with individuals, organizations and government to bring education, healthcare and innovation to those in need through development projects.

  • Vision: A thriving, educated, innovative and healthy society.
  • Mission: Deliver practical projects in partnership with all stakeholders through education, healthcare and innovation.

Our Programs

Education Support

School materials, scholarships and teacher training for rural schools.

Healthcare

Community health outreach, screenings and maternal care support.

Innovation

Introducing new approach to solving daily challenges through innovative ways.
